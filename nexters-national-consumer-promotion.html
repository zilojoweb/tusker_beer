National Consumer Promotion

How to participate in the promotion:

  • Purchase any of the following bottled promotional products of Tusker™ including Tusker Lager 500ml bottle, Tusker Cider 500ml bottle, Tusker Ndimu 300ml bottle, Tusker Lite 300ml bottle, and Tusker Malt 300ml bottled products;
  • Check under the crown of the above participating products to find an 8-character alphanumeric code;
  • Send the 8-digit alphanumeric code to 20770, via SMS message; and
  • Complete a one-off registration process to validate your code.
  • Thereafter, you will have an opportunity to share your message of cheer/support directly to Team Kenya in Paris.

What rewards will consumers receive in the promotion?

Consumers stand a chance to win a share of the following exciting prizes in Daily, Weekly and Monthly draws:

  • 1,000 daily winners of Kes 300 instant cash via mobile money.
  • 700 weekly winners of Kes 1,500 electronic voucher of a 6pack of Tusker Lager 500ml Can.
  • 10 monthly winners of Kes 150,000 worth of a home entertainment kitty (Comprising of a TV worth Kes 120,000 and a TV subscription service up to Kes 30,000 from authorised dealers).
