OUR ROOTS: NDOVU NI KUU

We are TUSKER. Kenya's original beer. A source of identity and pride since 1922. A beer renowned throughout our region and the world over. Our elephant logo stands apart - a familiar shorthand for who we are, where we have come from, and how far we plan to travel. We live in bright colors of yellow and black. The colors of sunshine and our soil. We pride ourselves in distinction and quality & we continue to stand tall as EABL's flagship brand.

THE BREW: FOAM NI GANI?

Brewed using Kenya's finest ingredients, when you think about Tusker, you have to think about Kenya. Tusker is as Kenyan as it can get. It's our gold liquid that embodies what makes us truly unique. Every bottle has carefully selected ingredients that revel in the magic that makes them right for each other oozing great heritage that dates back over 100yrs ago. Crowned by refined refreshment, it has never changed over the years. Every sip is refreshing, and the taste is perfectly balanced. A beer that goes beyond the liquid and into our souls. It is available in a 500ml amber-colored bottle, a 500ml Can, and 30/50-liter keg.
