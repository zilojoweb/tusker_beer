EVENTS


FIND WHERE THE FUN'S AT


Tusker is no stranger to hosting some of the most exciting and unforgettable events in Kenya. We are talking about events that bring people together to celebrate the good things in life. From beer festivals to music concerts and in-bar activations, Tusker experiences are a staple in the Kenyan entertainment scene. Quoting many of our consumers, "With Tusker, it’s always a VIBE!"

For us, it's more than just fun and games. The brand takes pride in leveraging these events to support and elevate the next generation of talent through the Tusker Nexters platform. And that's not all, if you're a fan of rugby, you surely remember the glorious Tusker Safari Sevens from back in the day. It was always a 'don’t miss out' kind of experience that brought together rugby enthusiasts from all over the country to support the game. How about the Tusker FC football experiences that leave the revelers yearning for more year after year?

To top it up, we can't forget to mention the Tusker Fest & Tusker meat-ups in-bar activations held in various towns and bars countrywide, featuring Nexter DJs and music artists. These are a must-experience for those looking for meaningful connection moments na mabeshte.

And if you are looking to experience a vibrant celebration of Kenyan culture, look no further than the Tusker OktobaFest! This highly anticipated event, held every October, draws crowds from all over to revel in the sights, sounds, and tastes of Kenya. But it's not just the attendees who benefit from the festival - local businesses also reap big rewards from their collaborations with the Tusker brand.

We have also supported the UHAI Festival in the recent past (a sports & arts festival for persons living with disabilities held on 24th March 2023 at the Masinde Muliro University in the Western Kenya region), showing our commitment to promoting and preserving the rich traditions of Kenya with an inclusive and diverse mindset.

By championing the Kenyan spirit of togetherness, Tusker elevates EABL's agenda to harness the power of diverse perspectives and ideas that drive innovation and growth and highlights Kenya’s budding talent while at it. So, what are you waiting for? Join the Tusker community and experience unforgettable moments na Mbogi!
