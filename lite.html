What happens when we craft a unique recipe of barley, cornstarch and hops? Perfection in the form of a low-calorie brew that can’t be taken LITEly.

Our unique brewing craft brings forth a light, golden-coloured liquid with a delightful aroma which appeals to the senses. Every sip reveals a light body that is heavy on taste, creating an enjoyable drinking experience.

It’s perfect for those who want to enjoy life, and live it to the fullest. This means having fun with friends and creating memories without feeling tired or weighed down after the hangouts.

Tusker Lite is best enjoyed chilled between 3°C and 7°C. Be it in a green coloured 330ml bottle, or a 500ml can, Tusker Lite will tantalize your taste buds and tingle the senses with its crispness.
